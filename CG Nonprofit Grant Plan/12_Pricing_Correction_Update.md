## CRITICAL PRODUCT CLARIFICATIONS

### Asymmetric Payment Model (UNIQUE VALUE PROP)

**How it works:**
- Parent A can have **Web Starter (FREE)**
- Parent B can have **Plus ($17.99)** or **Complete ($34.99)**
- Both parents can interact, send messages, set up exchanges, make fund requests
- The paying parent gets additional features (automation, exports, GPS, KidsCom)

**Examples:**

**Scenario 1: Free + Free**
- Both parents: ARIA flagging only (manual), basic calendar, web access only
- No automation, no exports, no mobile apps

**Scenario 2: Free + Plus**
- Free parent: ARIA flagging, basic calendar, web access
- Plus parent: Automated schedules, PDF exports, mobile app, 1 circle contact
- **Both can communicate and manage basics, but Plus parent gets automation**

**Scenario 3: Free + Complete**
- Free parent: Basic features
- Complete parent: GPS verification, KidsCom for their kids, court exports, priority support
- **Free parent benefits from better documentation even without paying**

**Scenario 4: Plus + Complete**
- Plus parent: Automation, exports, mobile, 1 circle
- Complete parent: All Plus features + GPS, KidsCom, court packages, priority support
- **Optimal for high-conflict situations**

**Marketing angle:**
> "Your ex doesn't have to pay for you to benefit from CommonGround. Start free, upgrade when you need more."

### FAQ Updates

**Q: Do both parents need to pay?**
A: No! One parent can use Web Starter (free forever) while the other pays for Plus or Complete. You'll both be able to communicate, manage exchanges, and track expenses. The paying parent just gets additional features like automation, mobile apps, and court-ready exports.

**Q: What's the difference between Plus and Complete?**
A: Plus ($17.99) is perfect for parents who want automation and mobile access. Complete ($34.99) adds GPS-verified exchanges, custody analytics, KidsCom video calls, and court-ready evidence packages—essential for high-conflict situations or active court cases.

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you're going to court, upgrade to Complete for the month you need evidence packages, then downgrade after. No penalties.

**Q: What if my ex won't pay?**
A: They don't have to. They can use Web Starter (free) and you can use Plus or Complete. The platform works asymmetrically—both parents can participate regardless of who pays.

**Q: Is there a family discount?**
A: The pricing is already per-family, not per-parent. If both parents want Premium features, each would subscribe individually to their preferred tier. But remember: one parent can always stay on Web Starter (free) while the other upgrades.

**Q: What happens after the 14-day trial?**
A: If you don't cancel, you'll be charged the monthly rate for your chosen plan. If you cancel during the trial, you won't be charged anything. You can always restart with Web Starter (free) if you want to keep using basic features.

## ANNUAL PRICING (RECOMMENDATION)

### Should You Offer Annual Discounts?

**Pros:**
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn (committed for year)
- Standard practice in SaaS

**Cons:**
- Co-parenting situations change quickly
- Users may feel locked in
- Custody arrangements often modified

**Recommendation:**
**Offer annual pricing with monthly escape hatch:**

**Plus:**
- Monthly: $17.99/month
- Annual: $179.99/year ($14.99/month - 17% off)
- Guarantee: "Cancel anytime for prorated refund"

**Complete:**
- Monthly: $34.99/month
- Annual: $349.99/year ($29.16/month - 17% off)
- Guarantee: "Cancel anytime for prorated refund"

**Why this works:**
- Encourages commitment (cash flow benefit)
- Removes fear of being stuck (refund guarantee)
- Standard 17% annual discount (SaaS best practice)
