# CommonGround Pricing Strategy & Competitive Analysis

## Executive Summary

**Recommended Pricing Model:** Hybrid freemium with nonprofit grants, professional licensing tiers, and case-based vouchers.

**Target Revenue Mix (Year 1):**
- Consumer subscriptions: 40% ($280K)
- Professional subscriptions: 45% ($315K)
- Case vouchers: 10% ($70K)
- Transaction fees: 5% ($35K)
- **Total ARR Target: $700K**

## Competitive Landscape Analysis

### Direct Competitor Pricing (January 2026)

| Platform | Consumer Plans | Professional Plans | Key Features |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **OurFamilyWizard** | $99-199/parent/year ($8.25-16.58/mo) | $249/year per attorney | Calendar, messaging, expense tracking, court-admissible |
| **Talking Parents** | $9.99/mo or $119/year per parent | $15/mo per case | Basic messaging, calendar, expense log |
| **AppClose** | $12.99/mo per parent | $199/year per professional | Mobile-first, basic features |
| **coParenter** | $12.99/mo or $129/year per parent | $399/year unlimited cases | Mediation focus, calendar |
| **2Houses** | €9.99/mo per parent (~$10.80) | No professional tier | European-based, full features |

### Key Insights

1. **Consumer pricing averages $10-17/mo per parent** - market is comfortable with this range
2. **Professional pricing is all over the map** - $15/mo to $399/year suggests no clear leader
3. **No competitor offers AI mediation** - this is our unique value, can command premium
4. **Most charge per parent** - creates barrier when one parent won't pay
5. **Annual discounts standard** - 15-25% off for annual commitment

---

## CommonGround Pricing Strategy

### Core Principles

1. **Remove barriers for DV survivors** - Grant program ensures safety not tied to payment ability
2. **Align law firm incentives** - More cases = more revenue, but manageable
3. **Create upgrade path** - Free trials convert to paid, paid converts to premium
4. **Fair split model** - Unlike competitors, we don't charge both parents full price

## Consumer Pricing (B2C)

### Freemium Model

**Free Tier: "CommonGround Basics"**
- **Price:** $0/month
- **Target:** Low-conflict co-parents, trial users
- **Features:**
  - Basic messaging (no ARIA)
  - Shared calendar
  - Up to 30 days message history
  - 1 child profile
  - Mobile app access
- **Limitations:**
  - No expense tracking
  - No agreement builder
  - No court exports
  - No professional access
- **Goal:** 1000+ free users creating platform awareness

**Paid Tier 1: "CommonGround Plus"**
- **Price:** $19.99/month per family ($239.88/year or $199/year annual)
- **Target:** Moderate conflict, organization-focused families
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Free
  - ARIA mediation (basic - up to 50 interventions/mo)
  - ClearFund expense tracking
  - Unlimited message history
  - Up to 3 child profiles
  - Basic agreement templates
  - Court-ready PDF exports
  - Email support
- **Conversion trigger:** When users hit ARIA limit or need expense tracking

**Paid Tier 2: "CommonGround Pro"** ⭐ MOST POPULAR
- **Price:** $39.99/month per family ($479.88/year or $399/year annual)
- **Target:** High-conflict cases, DV situations, complex custody
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Plus
  - Unlimited ARIA interventions
  - Advanced ARIA (context learning, predictive alerts)
  - Full SharedCare Agreement Builder (18 sections)
  - Silent Handoff mode
  - GPS exchange verification
  - Priority email support (24hr response)
  - Professional portal integration
  - Court verification with SHA-256 hashing
  - Compliance reporting
- **Conversion trigger:** High ARIA usage, attorney involvement, court case

**Paid Tier 3: "CommonGround Premium"**
- **Price:** $79.99/month per family ($959.88/year or $799/year annual)
- **Target:** Court-mandated, ultra high-conflict, wealthy families
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Pro
  - Dedicated customer success manager
  - White-glove onboarding
  - Custom agreement clauses
  - Advanced analytics dashboard
  - Multi-professional access (attorney + therapist + GAL)
  - KidComs with extended family circles
  - Unlimited document storage
  - Phone support
  - Quarterly case reviews
- **Conversion trigger:** Court order, attorney recommendation, income >$150K

### Special Programs

**CommonGround Cares: DV Survivor Grant**
- **Price:** FREE for 180 days, then $9.99/month (Pro features)
- **Eligibility:** Verified through nonprofit partner, restraining order, or DV advocate letter
- **Features:** Full Pro tier access
- **Goal:** 100 grant users in Year 1
- **Partner value:** Nonprofit gets co-branded impact reports

**Military Family Discount**
- **Discount:** 30% off all paid tiers
- **Verification:** ID.me integration
- **Price:** Plus $13.99, Pro $27.99, Premium $55.99

**Financial Hardship Program**
- **Sliding scale:** $9.99-29.99/month for Pro based on income
- **Verification:** Paystub or tax return
- **Goal:** Ensure access for all families

### Case Voucher System (Alternative Model)

For firms that want flexibility or seasonal practices:

**Voucher Packs:**
- 10 cases: $299 ($29.90/case)
- 25 cases: $699 ($27.96/case - 7% off)
- 50 cases: $1,299 ($25.98/case - 13% off)
- 100 cases: $2,399 ($23.99/case - 20% off)

**How it works:**
1. Firm purchases voucher pack
2. Assigns voucher to family file
3. Family gets Pro access for 90 days
4. Firm gets professional portal access for that case
5. After 90 days, family can convert to paid or firm buys new voucher

**Benefit:** Law firms control costs, families get temporary access, CommonGround gets revenue + usage data

- Value to nonprofit: $2,399 per family ($143,940 total value provided)
