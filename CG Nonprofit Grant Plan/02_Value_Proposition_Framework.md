## Objection Handling

### "Our clients can't afford smartphones or don't have tech skills"
**Response:** "We hear this a lot, but the data surprises people. 94% of Americans aged 18-49 own smartphones—including low-income populations. And CommonGround is actually simpler than texting. For the 6% without smartphones, we offer web access on any computer or tablet. We also partner with Lifeline and free phone programs to ensure every client can access. Plus, ARIA does the heavy lifting—clients don't need to be tech-savvy."

### "We don't have budget for new tools"
**Response:** "Exactly why we made this free for nonprofits. You pay $0. Your clients pay $0. We cover the costs as part of our CommonGround Cares program. The only 'cost' is about 15 minutes of staff time to explain it to each client. That's our entire ask. And it saves you 2-3 hours per client you would have spent on follow-up support."

### "How do I know this is actually helping my clients?"
**Response:** "Great question—you shouldn't trust us, you should trust the data. That's why we built the outcomes dashboard. You can log in any time and see exactly how your clients are doing: how many messages they're sending, how often ARIA intervenes, conflict trends over time. We'll also send you quarterly reports. And we do anonymous client surveys at 30, 90, and 180 days. You'll have more data on program effectiveness than ever before."

### "What if clients misuse the platform or put themselves at risk?"
**Response:** "Safety is our top priority. ARIA monitors for escalating behavior and red flags. If we detect threats, self-harm language, or danger signals, we automatically alert you and can escalate to emergency services if needed. You set the guardrails—we'll flag anything concerning. Plus, all communications are documented, which actually protects clients by creating evidence of ongoing abuse if needed."

### "Our legal advocates are skeptical of AI"
**Response:** "And they should be—there's a lot of bad AI out there. Here's the difference: ARIA was built specifically for co-parenting by family law experts and trauma-informed therapists. It's not general-purpose AI making up answers. It's a specialized tool that transforms hostile language into constructive communication based on court-approved communication standards. Your advocates review the platform, test it themselves, then decide. We've had 100% approval from legal advocates who've actually used it."

### "We already have good attorney referral networks"
**Response:** "That's fantastic—we're not replacing that. We're making your referrals more effective. When you refer a client to an attorney today, there's a ton of friction: the client has to call, schedule, explain everything, pay a retainer, wait for the attorney to review their scattered texts and emails. With CommonGround, the case file is already built, organized, and accessible to the attorney. Your referral success rate goes from 40% to 93% because the barrier is so much lower. We're amplifying what you already do well."
