Understanding Compliance

What is the Compliance Score?

The compliance score represents the percentage of financial obligations that were funded on time and in full. It is a key indicator of financial reliability and adherence to the support order.

Why it matters

Consistent funding ensures the child's needs—such as medical care, education, and extracurricular activities—are met without interruption. High compliance demonstrates a commitment to co-parenting stability. Discrepancies may arise from disputed charges or processing delays.
