# CommonGround Demo Personas - Southern California

## Overview

7 realistic co-parent situations representing different custody arrangements, conflict levels, and use cases for the CommonGround platform.

---

## Persona 1: Sarah & Mike (Cooperative Co-Parenting)

**Location:** Irvine, CA

**Background:**
- Married 10 years, divorced amicably 2 years ago
- Both remarried to supportive partners
- Professional careers (Sarah: Marketing Manager, Mike: Software Engineer)
- Focus: Organization and coordination, not conflict resolution

**Children:**
- Emma Williams (8) - 3rd grade, loves soccer and art
- Liam Williams (6) - 1st grade, into dinosaurs and Legos

**Custody Arrangement:**
- 50/50 split, alternating weeks (Week-On-Week-Off)
- Exchange: Sundays at 6pm
- Both parents live in Irvine (15 min apart)

**Platform Usage:**
- Shared calendar for activities
- ClearFund for soccer fees, art classes, school supplies
- Minimal ARIA interventions (cooperation score: 9/10)
- Quick Accord for holiday schedule adjustments
- Professional: Using family law attorney for agreement updates

**Financial:**
- No child support (equal income)
- Split expenses 50/50
- Combined income: $180k/year
