# CommonGround - Project Brief & Business Guide

> **For Claude Project Context** - This document provides comprehensive context for building, scaling, and operating CommonGround as a business.

## Executive Summary

**CommonGround** is an AI-powered co-parenting operating system that transforms high-conflict custody situations into collaborative partnerships. We serve the 50+ million Americans affected by divorce and custody arrangements annually, providing a neutral digital space where families can communicate, coordinate, and comply with custody agreements—all while protecting children from parental conflict.

### The Problem We Solve

- **$50B+ spent annually** on family law attorneys, much of it on preventable conflict
- **70% of custody cases** involve moderate-to-high conflict communication
- **Children exposed to parental conflict** show 2-3x higher rates of anxiety, depression, and behavioral issues
- **Courts are overwhelmed** with modification requests and compliance disputes
- **No single platform** addresses communication, scheduling, finances, and legal compliance together

### Our Solution

CommonGround provides a **unified co-parenting ecosystem** with:
- **ARIA (AI Relationship Intelligence Assistant)** - Real-time message mediation that transforms hostile communication into constructive dialogue
- **SharedCare Agreements** - Digital custody agreements that auto-generate schedules and track compliance
- **ClearFund** - Transparent expense sharing with court-admissible records
- **KidComs** - Safe, monitored communication channels for children
- **Court Portal** - Professional access for attorneys, mediators, and GALs
- **Verified Documentation** - SHA-256 hashed records for legal proceedings

### Traction & Validation

- Platform handles real custody cases with measurable conflict reduction
- ARIA intervention acceptance rate demonstrates user trust in AI mediation
- Professional Portal enables B2B revenue through legal practice integration
- Court-ready exports accepted in family court proceedings

## Product Overview

### Core Platform Features

#### 1. Family Files (Case Management Hub)
The central dashboard for each co-parenting relationship:
- Unified view of all case data, agreements, and communications
- Parent A/B role assignment with configurable permissions
- Children profiles with photos, medical info, and preferences
- Activity feed showing all case updates
- Quick actions for common tasks

#### 2. ARIA - AI Relationship Intelligence Assistant
Our flagship AI that mediates parent communication:

**How It Works:**
1. Parent composes message
2. ARIA analyzes for conflict patterns (hostility, blame, passive-aggression, etc.)
3. If flagged, ARIA suggests a rewritten version
4. Parent can accept, modify, or send original
5. All interventions tracked for compliance reporting

**Toxicity Levels:**
- 🟢 **Green** - Message OK to send
- 🟡 **Yellow** - Minor concerns, suggestion offered
- 🟠 **Orange** - Significant issues, strong recommendation to revise
- 🔴 **Red** - Severe content, sending blocked with explanation

**Key Differentiators:**
- Child-first framing in all suggestions
- Preserves sender's intent while removing inflammatory language
- Learns case context for personalized mediation
- Court-admissible intervention records

#### 3. SharedCare Agreement Builder
Digital custody agreement wizard with 18 comprehensive sections:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Parent Information | Contact details, emergency info |
| Children | Names, DOBs, special needs |
| Legal Custody | Decision-making authority |
| Physical Custody | Primary residence, percentages |
| Parenting Schedule | Weekly/bi-weekly patterns |
| Holidays | Rotation schedules by year |
| Exchanges | Locations, times, protocols |
| Transportation | Responsibility assignments |
| Child Support | Payment amounts, methods |
| Medical | Insurance, decision authority |
| Education | School choice, involvement |
| Communication | Co-parent and child contact rules |
| Child Contact | Third-party communication |
| Travel | Domestic/international rules |
| Relocation | Notice requirements |
| Dispute Resolution | Mediation before court |
| Other Provisions | Custom terms |
| Review & Approval | Dual-parent signature |

**Agreement Features:**
- ARIA-assisted section completion with guided questions
- Version history with change tracking
- Dual-parent approval workflow
- Court-ready PDF generation
- Automatic schedule generation from terms

#### 4. Schedule & Custody Tracking
Comprehensive calendar and exchange management:
- Visual calendar with custody periods color-coded by parent
- Custody exchange scheduling with GPS verification
- Silent handoff mode for high-conflict exchanges (no direct contact)
- QR code check-in confirmation
- Parenting time statistics and compliance reports
- Integration with agreement schedule terms

#### 5. ClearFund (Financial Management)
Transparent expense tracking and sharing:
- Expense requests with receipt uploads
- Automatic splitting based on custody percentages
- Court-ordered obligation tracking
- Payment processing via Stripe
- Attestation system for expense verification
- Complete audit trail for court

#### 6. KidComs (Child Communication)
Safe channels for children to communicate:
- Video/voice calls via Daily.co
- Text chat with ARIA monitoring
- Circle management for approved contacts (grandparents, etc.)
- Parental controls and call logging
- Child wallet system for allowances
- Age-appropriate interfaces

#### 7. Court Portal (Professional Access)
For attorneys, mediators, GALs, and parenting coordinators:
- Case dashboard with compliance metrics
- Timeline view of all case activity
- ARIA intervention history and analytics
- Secure messaging with clients
- Evidence compilation with integrity verification
- Court event scheduling
- AI-assisted intake interviews

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Identity

**Brand Colors:**
- **Primary Sage** (#2C5F5D) - Trust, growth, neutrality
- **Amber Accent** (#D4A853) - Warmth, hope, ARIA's presence
- **Neutral Palette** - Soft grays and whites for calm UI

**Typography:**
- **Crimson Text** (Serif) - Headlines, lending authority and warmth
- **System Sans** - Body text for readability

**Design Principles:**
1. **Calm over chaotic** - Reduce visual stress for users in difficult situations
2. **Neutral territory** - No visual bias toward either parent
3. **Child-centric imagery** - Subtle reminders of the ultimate priority
4. **Professional credibility** - Court-worthy appearance
5. **Mobile-first** - Parents manage custody on-the-go

### User Experience Principles

1. **Reduce friction for good behavior** - Make constructive actions easy
2. **Add friction for destructive behavior** - Pause and reflect before conflict
3. **Celebrate cooperation** - Acknowledge when parents work together
4. **Protect children always** - Never expose kids to adult conflict
5. **Build habits** - Consistent patterns for check-ins, communication, tracking

## Sales Playbooks

### Playbook 1: Consumer Acquisition

#### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Recently divorced or separating (0-2 years)
- Children ages 3-16
- Moderate-to-high conflict relationship
- Tech-comfortable (smartphone primary device)
- Income $50K+ (can afford subscription)
- Values: Children's wellbeing, reducing legal costs

#### Discovery Questions
1. "How do you currently communicate with your co-parent about the kids?"
2. "What's the most frustrating part of coordinating custody?"
3. "How often do misunderstandings lead to conflict?"
4. "How much are you spending on attorney communication?"
5. "How do your children react to co-parent tension?"

#### Value Propositions by Pain Point

| Pain Point | Value Prop | Feature |
|------------|------------|---------|
| "Every text turns into a fight" | "ARIA transforms your messages before they cause conflict" | ARIA Mediation |
| "We can't agree on anything" | "Build agreements together with AI guidance" | Agreement Builder |
| "They never pay on time" | "Automated tracking and reminders" | ClearFund |
| "Exchanges are awkward" | "Contactless handoffs with verification" | Silent Handoff |
| "Lawyers cost too much" | "Reduce billable hours by 70%" | Self-Service Tools |

#### Objection Handling

**"It's too expensive"**
> "Most families spend $300/hour on attorney communication. One prevented conflict pays for months of CommonGround. Plus, our court-ready records often reduce legal fees significantly."

**"My ex won't use it"**
> "You can start using it solo—the benefits show immediately. We also offer resources for encouraging co-parent adoption, and many courts are now recommending or mandating platforms like ours."

**"We already use [competitor]"**
> "Unlike basic calendar apps, CommonGround actively prevents conflict with AI mediation. Our ARIA system has prevented thousands of hostile exchanges. Would you like to see how it transforms real messages?"

**"I don't trust AI with my custody case"**
> "ARIA suggests—you decide. Every message shows you the original and suggestion side-by-side. The AI is trained specifically for co-parenting by family law experts. And everything creates court-admissible records you control."
