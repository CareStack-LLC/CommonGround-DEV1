Welcome to CommonGround

We're here to help you navigate co-parenting with clarity and calm. Follow these simple steps to get started with your new secure family space.

1 Create Your Family File

Think of this as your secure digital home base. Setup your Family File to organize everything in one place—your children's profiles, shared expenses, and important documents. It’s the foundation for organized co-parenting.

2 Choose Your Agreement Path

Every situation is unique. Need a simple, temporary understanding? Use a QuickAccord for fast, flexible arrangements. Need a comprehensive, long-term plan? Build a SharedCare Agreement to cover every detail of your parenting schedule.

3 Manage Finances clearly

Stop the receipt chase. Use ClearFund Requests to track shared expenses transparently. Your personal **Parent Wallet** keeps everything organized, ensuring financial discussions stay factual and fair.

Meet Aria, Your Sentiment Shield

Communication can be hard. Aria is your always-on AI mediator that helps keep conversations constructive. It flags high-conflict language before you send it and blocks toxic messages to keep your peace of mind intact.
